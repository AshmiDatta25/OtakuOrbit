Oshi No Ko

Oshi no Ko follows the lives of Aqua and Ruby, twin children born to a famous idol, Ai Hoshino, who is tragically murdered. Reborn into the entertainment industry, they navigate the complexities of the idol world, grappling with their identity, the truth behind their mother's death, and the dark underbelly of the industry. As they grow older, Aqua and Ruby become determined to uncover the truth and seek justice, while also pursuing their own dreams in the spotlight.
