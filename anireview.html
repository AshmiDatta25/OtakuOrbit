Demon Slayer

Kimetsu no Yaiba follows Tanjiro Kamado, a young boy whose life is upended when his family is massacred by demons, leaving his sister Nezuko as the sole survivor, albeit transformed into one. Driven by revenge and hope, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, wielding a unique Nichirin sword. Under the tutelage of the Hashira, elite demon slayers, Tanjiro undergoes rigorous training. As he battles formidable demons, he uncovers a tragic history tied to Muzan Kibutsuji, the Demon King. Through sacrifice and camaraderie, Tanjiro and his companions strive to protect humanity and find a cure for Nezuko.
