Ouran High School Host Club

Ouran High School Host Club revolves around Haruhi Fujioka, a scholarship student mistaken for a boy due to her androgynous appearance. While searching for a quiet study spot, she stumbles upon the elite Ouran High School Host Club. To repay a debt for breaking a valuable vase, she becomes a host, entertaining female students. As Haruhi navigates the glamorous world of the Host Club, she forms unexpected bonds with its members, balancing her secret identity and academic life while experiencing the eccentricities of the wealthy and privileged.
