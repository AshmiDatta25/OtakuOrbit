Naruto

Naruto chronicles the life of Naruto Uzumaki, an adolescent ninja ostracized for being the host of a mischievous nine-tailed fox demon. Yearning for acceptance and recognition, Naruto trains relentlessly to become a Hokage, the leader of his village. Alongside friends Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, he embarks on perilous missions to protect the ninja world. As Naruto matures, he uncovers the dark secrets of his past, confronts powerful enemies, and ultimately fulfills his dream while making profound sacrifices for those he cares about.
